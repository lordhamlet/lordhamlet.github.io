Modern cities generate huge amount of data, and have all sorts of issues resulting from congestions and crowds. My research interests lie in extracting insights from such data, creating models and simulations that can serve as sandboxes for decision makers, and recommending actionable policies using advanced computational techniques from the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Operations Research.
